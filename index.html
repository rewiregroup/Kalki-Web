About us

Our History

In 2020, Kalki Corp embarked on a transformative journey in response to the pressing issues faced by the education sector during the corona pandemic. Recognizing the critical need for improved technical infrastructure, we set out to address these challenges head-on. Our founders, inspired by a shared vision, conceptualized the idea of developing a bare-minimum infrastructure solution for educational organizations. Little did we know that this initial vision would evolve into a groundbreaking software platform.

Over the course of nearly two years, our dedicated team poured their passion and expertise into crafting the fundamental version of our software. This arduous process involved extensive research, development, and rigorous testing, as we aimed to create a reliable and user-friendly solution that would empower educational institutions to adapt and thrive in the digital landscape.

Today, our software stands as a testament to our commitment to educational advancement, providing institutions with the essential tools they need to deliver quality education, connect with students, and navigate the challenges of the modern world. As we continue to grow and innovate, our mission remains unwavering: To enhance education through technology, making learning accessible and efficient for all.
