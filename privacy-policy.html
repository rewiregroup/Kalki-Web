Privacy and policy

Welcome to our privacy and policy page for our ERP platform for schools. At Rewire Group, we take the privacy and security of our users very seriously. We understand that schools entrust us with sensitive information about their students, teachers, and staff, and it is our responsibility to protect that information. This page outlines our privacy and policy practices for our ERP platform, including how we collect, use, and share personal information. We encourage you to read this page carefully and contact us if you have any questions or concerns.

Collection of personal information:

We collect personal information from our users when they sign up for our ERP platform or use certain features of the platform. This information may include name, email address, phone number, and other personal details. We may also collect information about how our users use the platform, such as which pages they visit and which features they access.

Use of personal information:

We use the personal information we collect from our users to provide them with access to the platform and its features, to communicate with them about their account or the platform, and to improve the platform based on user feedback. We may also use personal information for marketing and analytics purposes, such as to send users newsletters or to understand how the platform is being used.

Sharing of personal information:

We do not sell or share personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes. We may share personal information with third parties who provide services on our behalf, such as hosting or analytics services. These third parties are required to protect the privacy and security of the personal information they receive from us and are not permitted to use it for their own marketing purposes.

Security:

We take steps to protect the personal information we collect from our users against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We use encryption, secure servers, and other security measures to protect against unauthorized access to personal information.
